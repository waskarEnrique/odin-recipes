Tacos Al Pastor

Description

Tacos al Pastor are a beloved Mexican dish, featuring marinated pork cooked in the style of shawarma. They are traditionally served with fresh pineapple, onion, cilantro, and a variety of salsas for an explosion of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 500g of pork, thinly sliced
  • 3 dried guajillo chilies
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup of pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Corn tortillas
  • Fresh pineapple chunks
  • Chopped onion
  • Fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Soak the guajillo chilies in hot water until soft.
  2. Blend the chilies with garlic, pineapple juice, vinegar, oregano, cumin, and salt into a sauce.
  3. Marinate the pork in the sauce for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
  4. Cook the marinated pork in a hot skillet until browned.
  5. Serve in warm tortillas with pineapple, onion, and cilantro.
