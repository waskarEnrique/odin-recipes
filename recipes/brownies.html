Brownies

Description

These rich and fudgy brownies are a chocolate lover’s dream, with a dense texture and intense flavor. This recipe is simple yet satisfying, producing moist brownies perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 150g butter
  • 200g sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100g flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a baking pan.
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together in a double boiler.
  3. Stir in the sugar, followed by the eggs one at a time.
  4. Sift in the flour and salt, and mix until combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, until just set.
  6. Let cool before slicing and serving.
