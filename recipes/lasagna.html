Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an iconic Italian comfort food, featuring layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This hearty dish is perfect for family gatherings or a satisfying meal any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g of ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 800g of tomato sauce
  • 250g of ricotta cheese
  • 200g of mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 50g of Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a large pan, sauté the onion and garlic, then add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce to the beef and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, add a layer of noodles, then spread ricotta cheese, followed by mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  6. Finish with a layer of sauce and sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden and bubbling. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
